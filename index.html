Background

We Are Grow Up.

Grow Up is a full-service marketing firm, offering a wide range of below-the-line marketing solutions. We enhance clients’ multichannel marketing through integrated O2O (Online-to-Offline) strategies.

With our clients, we focus on how we can provide them with solutions and business opportunities, rather than just 'pushing' single services. Our team consists of diverse backgrounds in business, marketing, advertising, and multimedia design. It's the passion that brings us together and enables us to delivery leading projects.

Our Mission

To be our clients' consistent strategic partner in total below-the-line communications and O2O marketing. We connect brands with customers directly through seamless integration of brand and trade marketing services.
